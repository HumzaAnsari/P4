What is LFG

LFG is a Looking For Group website designed to help others find other players to play with in games. You can create a post asking for people to message you on platforms like discord or twitter, specify how many people you want, what minimum level you want them to be, and what ever else you could think of to include in your post. You can also go through other peoples post and look for one that fits your needs. Good luck and remember to play nice.
